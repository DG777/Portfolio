What's up? I'm Matvey (Matthew) Tarchutkin, an 18 y.o. Russian aspiring designer and web-developer, shower philosopher and an all-round guy, who is interested in lots of things and tries out everything he could lay his hands on. Why specialize in one thing when you can know a bit of everything?

Currently studying Bachelor of Creative Technologies (BCT) at the Auckland University of Technologies (AUT) and loving every minute of it. Having always been interested in design I've never studied it formally, until now; and it's not just design that interests me: I'm pretty passionate about modern technologies, the Web, music, traveling, sustainable living and self-education, also have interests in fitness, philosophy and pharmacology. I see myself mixing all of that in my future works, somehow, don't even know if it's possible yet.

Since moving half across the world last year to New Zealand I've had experiences I'll never forget, acquired skills I never thought I would and met a ridiculous amount of most interesting people. Completely changing everything in your life is totally worth it, really.

Alright, so my biggest influence is dream-pop and shoegaze music with it's cloudy textures, hazy ethereal sounds and pastel colours (hence the site's palette). But I try getting it from everywhere I can, as any artist should really.

And I like tea.
