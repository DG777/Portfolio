Line Racer // 2015

The fast-dealing, crazy card game, where you can race your mates head-to-head. Lay your cards. Build your line. Win the cup.

In the Box

144 cards including: 4 sets of 36 cards in red, blue, green and yellow - each set containing: 1x Go Card, 1x Stop Card, 1x Cup Card, 1x Wait 10sec Card, 2x Bridge Cards, 30x Line Cards.

The Players Goal

To create a pathway from go card to finishing cup cards, competing against each player to reach the cup first.

The Play Outcome

The players should feel the competitiveness of the game. Whether they are playing multiplayer or individually, they should feel the pressure of placing the cards down strategically and as fast as possible. In multiplayer, the players should feel excited and feel the fast paced adrenaline-filled atmosphere.
