Modern dreamers

Boldness. Hallucination. And a bunch of one-point perspective videos. Started out as just a suit, evolved into a full-blown installation. The video is projected onto a corner between 2 walls, the actor is moving inside of that space, coming up to bystanders, closely looking into their eyes and breathing heavily. Reflections from glass shards in your eyes. Explores the emotion of confusion and hallucination.
